Problem Statement: Outfit App

Many people struggle with putting together outfits that both look and feel good. Others struggle with putting together new outfits and not repeating old ones. An outfit can look great but not feel great on you or adhere to your exact style preferences. Our app would allow you to obtain your ideal clothing aesthetic using the articles of clothing you already possess.

Affinity Diagram: Outfit App
